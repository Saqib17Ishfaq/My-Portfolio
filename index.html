Project 1: Diary Farm Management System

A Dairy Farm Management System (DFMS) is a software tool that helps manage and optimize dairy farm operations. It tracks livestock, milk production, feeding, breeding, health, inventory, and finances. By automating tasks and providing real-time insights, DFMS improves productivity, reduces costs, and supports better decision-making for more efficient and profitable dairy farming.

Project 2: Interactive Story Game

An Interactive Story Game is a type of video game where players make choices that influence the narrative, characters, and outcomes. These games combine storytelling with gameplay, allowing players to actively shape the direction of the plot through decisions, actions, and dialogues. The choices made can lead to multiple story paths and endings, creating a dynamic, personalized experience that encourages replayability and emotional engagement.
